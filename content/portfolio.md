## ApeX Omni - Decentralized Perpetual Trading Platform

**[Visit ApeX Omni →](https://omni.apex.exchange/trade/BTCUSDT)**

### Overview
A sophisticated decentralized exchange for perpetual futures trading, enabling traders to access leveraged positions on crypto assets with advanced risk management features.

### Key Features
- **Perpetual Contracts** - Trade BTC, ETH, and 100+ crypto assets with up to 50x leverage
- **Cross-Collateral Margin** - Use multiple assets as collateral with optimized margin efficiency
- **Advanced Order Types** - Limit, market, and conditional orders with TP/SL automation
- **Real-Time Analytics** - TradingView charts, order book depth, and funding rate tracking
- **Omnichain Architecture** - Built on zkLink technology for cross-chain liquidity aggregation

### Technical Stack
- **Smart Contracts:** Solidity, zkSync
- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, TradingView Charts
- **Backend:** High-performance order matching engine
- **Blockchain:** Ethereum, zkLink Omnichain
- **Security:** Multi-signature wallets, automated risk management
